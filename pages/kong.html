Kong's Facebook Games (2024)

From February to April 2024, I took up an Intern Game Developer position at Kong Software in Hanoi, a small game company that targets the Facebook Instant platform to publish their products.

During my time, I worked on the following projects:

  • Drop Number 2048
  • Hamster Adventure: Draw to Save
  • Shape Block Puzzle

Image: Games published


Drop Number 2024:

A 2048 merge puzzle game, my test project, finished and published in 2 weeks. Team of 2: developer and artist.

Hamster Adventure: Draw to Save:

A physics puzzle game where you draw a line to save a hamster. Took a month to be finished and published. Team of 3: developer, designer, and artist.
