Azrael (2024)

Azrael is a First-Person Arena Shooter inspired by the likes of Warhammer 40,000: Boltgun (2023) & Ultrakill (2020). The game is based loosely on the idea that the first angel, i.e. Azrael, has to cleanse Earth of its demonic inhabitants before God create humanity.

The gameplay is focused on its variety of gunplay and special movement ability, allowing for a feeling of freedom and satisfaction upon shooting your enemies.

This was a Junior-Senior Collaborative Project for my Computer Game Design And Programming degree at British University of Vietnam. The team includes:

  • Le Chi Duong - Project Manager, Programmer, 3D Animator
  • Nguyen Quang Huy- Lead Programmer
  • Can Cong Vinh (me) - Programmer, SFXs, Music
  • Ha Hung Phong - Programmer, Map Designer
  • Trieu Le Viet Bao - Programmer, Map Designer
