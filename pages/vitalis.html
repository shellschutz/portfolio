Vitalis (2026- Ongoing)

Vitalis is a TTRPG-inspired Roguelike and Dungeon Crawler where you try to escape an occult forest. Cast your die to move, fight, and flee. Scale your character, collect items, and weapons. Defeat the boss and leave the forest, or run away and bide your time.
