Fragile (2025) [Link]

Fragile is a point-and-click adventure game immersing players into the shattered lives of a group of college students coming to an end, as each go their separate ways after their school year ends.

This was the debut project of pngTriads, our indie development team. The team includes:

  • legendofnothing - Programmer, not over it award.
  • OperatorPrime- Game Designer, Narrative Designer, magic card guy.
  • shellschutz (me) - Artist, Sound Designer, Ink Specialist, reincarnated, he was stargazing, life goes on...

Contributions:

Art Style

My main role for this project was 2D artist, I was responsible for the game's art style.

It's a very minimalistic, sketchy art style, aims to look like an old amateur web comic. It has a very limited color palette that helps highlights lighting contrasts in scenes, which helps accentuates the feeling of isolation or loneliness. Also a major inspiration for composition is Edward Hopper, whose painting expresses similar feelings.

Sound Design

Just like with art style, the sound design was very minimalistic, no background music at all, instead it is deafeningly quiet. Again the intention is isolation and loneliness. Sounds effects are mostly realistic sound clips of actions happening in the story (i.e. knocking on a door, or footstep here and there, etc.)

However at the end of the game there is a small piece of music composed by me, which is to be the cinematic ending to the game, breaking the quiet atmosphere at once.

Track: Melody - Ending Track (Could Contain Spoilers)


Ink Scripting

I introduced the team to Inkle's Ink scripting language, specifically used for narrative scripting. I helped edit Ink scripts to implement things such as Sound Effect and score keeping for certain events.
